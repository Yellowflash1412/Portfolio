PkankuzziGame for elderly people

Python & AI

We intend to boost cognitive skills and stimulate learning, deriving inspiration from a traditional game called Pallankuzhi which is known to enhance math and memory skills.

Pallanghuzi, or Pallankuli (பல்லாங்குழி in Tamil,"सत्कोलि" or Satkoli in Marathi), is a traditional ancient mancala game played in South India especially Tamil Nadu and Kerala. Later the games were spread to other places including Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh in India as well as, Sri Lanka and Malaysia. The game is played by two players, with a wooden board that has fourteen pits in all, hence the name from the words fourteen pits (pathinaalam kuzhi). There have been several variations in the layout of the pits, one among them being seven pits on each player's side. The pits contain cowry shells, seeds or small pebbles used as counters. There are several variations of the game depending on the number of shells each player starts with.

Maintaining mental fitness as you get older is just as vital as keeping your body healthy and strong. Our brain is in charge of keeping us happy throughout our lives, and as we become older, mental health is a crucial factor in our ability to live freely. Learning about the latest gadgets and technologies is crucial in the ever-evolving world of technology. The elderly population, however, is unfamiliar with and disinterested in technology.
